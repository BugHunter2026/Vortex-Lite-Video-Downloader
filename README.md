🚀 Concept: Safety by Design
Unlike unrestricted downloaders, Vortex Lite follows a strict compliance-first approach. It is ideal for family or professional environments where only moderated social content should be accessed.

Curated Whitelist: Access is strictly limited to YouTube, Instagram, Vimeo, and TikTok.

Automatic Blocking: Any attempt to process links from unsupported or unverified domains is automatically blocked.

Batch Intelligence: Analyze and queue up to 10 URLs simultaneously.

Visual Control:

Resolution scaling from standard quality up to 4K Ultra HD.

Customizable bitrates and framerate settings.

Audio Precision:

Adjustable sample rates and channel modes (Mono/Stereo).

Live Previews: Automatically fetches video titles and thumbnails during analysis.
